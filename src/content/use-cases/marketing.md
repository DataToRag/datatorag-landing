## How marketing teams use Claude

Marketing teams juggle campaigns, content, analytics, and client communication daily. Claude with the right integrations becomes your always-available marketing assistant.

### Campaign & CRM Management

Connect Salesforce to manage campaigns and track leads through conversation.

> **"Show me all leads from the last webinar that haven't been contacted"**
>
> **"Update the status of the Acme campaign to 'In Progress'"**

### Content & Documents

With Google Drive, Claude can find and analyze your content library, brand guidelines, and campaign briefs.

> **"Find our brand guidelines doc and list the approved color codes"**
>
> **"Summarize the key points from last month's campaign report"**

### Team Coordination

The Slack integration helps you keep up with fast-moving marketing channels.

> **"What did the design team say about the new landing page in Slack?"**

### Email Management

Google Workspace integration lets Claude help triage client emails and draft responses.

> **"Summarize all emails from the client about the product launch"**
