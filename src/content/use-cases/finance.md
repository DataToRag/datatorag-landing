## How finance teams use Claude

Finance professionals deal with complex spreadsheets, compliance documents, and reporting deadlines. Claude with the right integrations helps you work smarter.

### Financial Analysis & Reporting

Connect Google Drive to let Claude analyze spreadsheets and documents directly.

> **"Open the Q3 financial report and compare revenue to Q2"**
>
> **"Summarize the key risks from the latest audit document"**

### CRM & Revenue Tracking

With Salesforce, Claude can pull pipeline data and revenue metrics on demand.

> **"What's our total closed revenue this quarter?"**
>
> **"List all overdue invoices over $10k"**

### Email & Calendar

Google Workspace integration helps manage the constant flow of financial communications.

> **"Find all emails about the year-end close from the accounting team"**
>
> **"When is the next board meeting?"**
