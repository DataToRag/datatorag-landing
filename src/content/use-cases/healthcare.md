## How healthcare teams use Claude

Healthcare professionals spend hours on documentation, research, and administrative tasks. Claude with the right integrations can dramatically reduce that burden — without compromising patient privacy.

### Documentation & Notes

Connect Claude to your Google Drive or document storage to instantly find policies, protocols, and reference materials.

> **"Find our infection control protocol and summarize the key steps"**

Instead of digging through folders, Claude searches your documents and gives you the answer in seconds.

### Team Communication

With the Slack integration, Claude can help you stay on top of team communications without constant context-switching.

> **"Summarize what the nursing team discussed this week in Slack"**

### Scheduling & Coordination

Connect Google Workspace to let Claude help with calendar management and email triage.

> **"Do I have any meetings with the pharmacy team this week?"**
