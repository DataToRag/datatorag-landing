## Why healthcare teams are using Claude

Healthcare professionals spend up to 50% of their time on administrative tasks — documentation, searching for protocols, coordinating with team members, and managing schedules. Claude with the right integrations can cut that dramatically.

## The 3 integrations every healthcare team should start with

### 1. Google Workspace — for emails and scheduling

**Setup time:** 10 minutes | **Difficulty:** Beginner

This is the easiest win. Connect your work email and calendar to Claude:

- Triage patient-related emails quickly
- Check your schedule between appointments
- Draft professional responses to administrative emails

[Set up Google Workspace integration →](/integrations/google-workspace-mcp)

### 2. Google Drive — for policies and protocols

**Setup time:** 10 minutes | **Difficulty:** Beginner

Connect your document storage so Claude can find information instantly:

- Look up infection control protocols
- Find HR policies and compliance documents
- Search through training materials

[Set up Google Drive integration →](/integrations/google-drive-mcp)

### 3. Slack — for team coordination

**Setup time:** 5 minutes | **Difficulty:** Beginner

If your team uses Slack for communication:

- Catch up on what happened during your shift
- Find specific conversations about patient care plans
- Get summaries of team discussions

[Set up Slack integration →](/integrations/slack-mcp)

## Important: Privacy and compliance

Claude processes your queries securely, but always be mindful:

- **Never share PHI (Protected Health Information)** in chat queries unless your organization has specifically approved it
- Check with your compliance team before connecting work tools
- Use integrations for administrative and operational tasks, not clinical decision-making

## Next steps

1. Start with **one integration** — we recommend Google Workspace
2. Try it for a week and see how it fits your workflow
3. Add more integrations as you get comfortable
