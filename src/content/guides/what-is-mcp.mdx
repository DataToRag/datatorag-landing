## The short version

**MCP stands for Model Context Protocol.** It's a way to connect Claude to the apps you already use — like Slack, Google Drive, Salesforce, and more.

Think of it like installing an app on your phone. Your phone can do basic things on its own, but when you install apps, it becomes much more useful. MCP integrations work the same way for Claude.

## What can Claude do with integrations?

Without integrations, Claude can only work with what you type or paste into the chat. With integrations, Claude can:

- **Read your emails** and draft replies
- **Search your documents** and answer questions about them
- **Check your calendar** and help schedule meetings
- **Look up customer data** in your CRM
- **Post messages** to Slack channels

And that's just the beginning.

## Do I need to be technical?

**Not at all.** Most integrations take 5-10 minutes to set up. We provide step-by-step guides with screenshots for every integration. If you can install an app on your phone, you can set up an MCP integration.

## How do I get started?

1. **Download Claude Desktop** from [claude.ai/download](https://claude.ai/download)
2. **Browse our integrations** at [/integrations](/integrations) and pick one that matches your work
3. **Follow the setup guide** — each integration page has a step-by-step walkthrough
4. **Start chatting** — just ask Claude to do things with your connected apps
