## You're probably underusing Claude

If you're only using Claude to ask questions and write text, you're missing out. By connecting Claude to the tools you already use, it goes from a helpful chatbot to a genuine work assistant.

Here are 5 things you can do today.

## 1. Triage your inbox in seconds

**Integration:** Google Workspace

Instead of scanning through dozens of emails:

> "Show me the 5 most important emails from today and summarize each one"

Claude reads your inbox, identifies the important ones, and gives you a quick summary. You decide what needs a response — Claude can draft those too.

## 2. Get instant answers from your documents

**Integration:** Google Drive

Stop digging through folders looking for that one document:

> "What does our refund policy say about returns after 30 days?"

Claude searches your Drive, finds the relevant document, and gives you the answer with a reference to the source.

## 3. Stay on top of team chats

**Integration:** Slack

When you've been away from Slack for a few hours:

> "Summarize what I missed in #general and #project-alpha today"

Claude reads through the messages and gives you the highlights — decisions made, questions asked, action items assigned.

## 4. Manage your CRM without clicking

**Integration:** Salesforce

Instead of navigating Salesforce dashboards:

> "Show me all deals closing this month that are still in negotiation"

Claude queries your CRM and presents the data in a clear format. You can even ask follow-up questions about specific deals.

## 5. Never miss a meeting

**Integration:** Google Workspace

Start your day with:

> "What's on my calendar today? Flag any meetings I need to prepare for"

Claude checks your schedule and highlights meetings where you might need to review materials beforehand.
